Disse to sæt queries, efter hinanden, merger først ens-lydende marknoder der udspringer fra en node med et givent ID. 
Dernæst samler den relationerne til de mergede noder, tsammen til 1.


MATCH (n:MARK)<-[:Mark]-(m) WHERE ID(m)=303
WITH n.name AS name, COLLECT(n) AS nodelist, COUNT(*) AS count
WHERE count > 1
CALL apoc.refactor.mergeNodes(nodelist) YIELD node

MATCH (n:MARK)<-[r:Mark]-(m) WHERE ID(m)=303
WITH n.name AS name, COLLECT(r) as rels, COUNT(*) AS count
CALL apoc.refactor.mergeRelationships(rels) YIELD rel
MATCH (n:MARK)<-[r:Mark]-(m) WHERE ID(m)=303
RETURN rel
